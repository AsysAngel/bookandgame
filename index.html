Books I Recommend


  • Title: The Beginning After the End
  • Author: TurtleMe
  • Genre: Fantasy Fiction, High Fantasy, Fairy tale
  • Description: King Grey was once the strongest man in the world. After dying mysteriously in his bed, he woke up as a baby with a new name and a loving family. Arthur Leywin (formerly King Grey) must learn to control his newfound powers and protect the ones he loves at all cost.

  • Title: The Author's POV
  • Author: Entrail_Ji
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Romance, Comedy
  • Description: The protagonist, a failed author who was orphaned at 14, wakes up inside his own novel. Ren Dover (the person whom the author woke up as) is an extra in his own novel and he has to figure out how to grow stronger and save the world from the destruction of demons.


Video Games I Love


  • Title: The Last of Us
  • Developer: Naughty Dog
  • Genre: Action-Adventure, Apocolyptic, Zombie
  • Description: The Last of Us is a third-person perspective game where the player traverses the post-apocolyptic world as Joel twenty years after the world ended and his daughter was shot and killed by a soldier. Joel is tasked with escorting a young girl, Ellie who is immune to the zombie virus across the United States to develop a cure for the zombie inducing virus from cordyceps. Joel and Ellie must survive in harsh conditions against zombies and humans alike.

  • Title: Uncharted 4: A Theif's End
  • Developer: Naughty Dog
  • Genre: Action-Adventure, Third-person shooter
  • Description: Players control Nathan Drake, a retired treasure hunter coaxed out of retirement by his presumed dead brother Samuel. With the help of his long time friend, Victor Sullivan, they set out on an adventure to find Henry Avery's long lost treasure.
